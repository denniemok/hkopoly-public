HKOPOLY is a free, browser-based Monopoly-style board game with authentic Hong Kong neighbourhoods, from Sham Shui Po to Yuen Long.
No download, no account. Open a link, pick a nickname, and play with friends. Inspired by Richup.io.
💚 No ads. No paywalls. No VIP tiers.
A community-first passion project. Always free to play.

✨ Features

💚 100% Free
No ads, paywalls, or hidden fees.
🎮 Instant Play
Zero downloads or sign-ups. Jump straight into a room.
📱 Mobile-Friendly
Responsive UI for phones and tablets.
🏘️ Localised Maps
Urban and New Territories & Islands boards out of the box.
📜 Complete Ruleset
Doubles, jail, Chance/Community Chest, mortgages, and auctions.
⚡ Real-Time Multiplayer
2 to 10 players with live boards, chat, and game logs.
🤝 Flexible Trading
Trade properties, cash, and Get Out of Jail cards. Skip a purchase to trigger a timed auction.
💡 Trade Hints
Valuation context to help negotiations. Toggle off in the lobby for a tougher game.
🤖 Smart Bots
Strategic AI that bids, trades, and manages debt when players are away.
🛫 Auto Pilot
Hand your seat to the AI on disconnect, or toggle it anytime.
👀 Spectate & Rejoin
Watch live matches or hot-swap back into your seat.
⚙️ Custom House Rules
Host controls starting cash, player caps, visibility, spectators, and more.
🔊 Immersive Audio
Custom SFX with browser-saved volume settings.
🌐 Fully Bilingual
English and Traditional Chinese (Hong Kong) via dedicated sites.

🧩 Quick Start

1️⃣ Create or join a room
Pick Join Game or Create Game on the homepage.
2️⃣ Set your name
Choose a display nickname for the room.
3️⃣ Roll & rule
Host picks a map and starts. On your turn: Roll → Buy / Pay Rent / Trade → End Turn.

🗺️ Board Maps

Two boards ship with the game today. Map data lives in the GitHub repo. Contributions welcome.

🗺️ Contribute a map
Fork the repo, add JSON under maps/en/ and maps/zh-HK/, then open a Pull Request.
